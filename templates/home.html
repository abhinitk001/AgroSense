What AgroSense Does

Everything your farm needs,
in one place

From soil sensors to satellite weather, AgroSense brings together every signal that matters for a complete picture of your farm's health.

Plant Disease Detection
Upload a photo and our AI identifies diseases, nutrient deficiencies, and pest damage with 96% accuracy — with exact treatment steps.
Live IoT Sensor Data
ESP32 nodes transmit NPK, TDS, pH, moisture, and temperature via WiFi/5G. Watch your soil's vital signs update in real time.
Hyperlocal Weather
Field-level forecasts, rain probability, and frost alerts — integrated with your planting calendar so you sow and harvest at the right time.

Getting Started

From sensors to insights in minutes

Setting up AgroSense takes under 30 minutes. No technical background needed.

1
Plant your sensors
Plug ESP32 nodes into your soil. They auto-connect to WiFi and immediately start streaming NPK, TDS, pH, temperature, and humidity data to your dashboard.
2
Tell us about your farm
Enter your location, farm size, and current crops. We pull in weather data and calibrate all recommendations to your specific region, soil type, and growing season.
3
Let AgroSense work
Your dashboard fills with live data. Our AI monitors everything 24/7 and sends you alerts, crop advice, and market signals — while you focus on farming.
IoT Integration
Your ESP32 speaks.
We listen — 24/7.

AgroSense integrates with ESP32 microcontrollers over any WiFi or 5G network. Sensor readings arrive at your dashboard within 2 seconds of measurement.

  • NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) soil sensors
  • TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) for water quality
  • Soil pH and electrical conductivity (EC)
  • Soil moisture and root-zone temperature
  • Ambient temperature and relative humidity
  • Updates every 30 seconds — offline buffering built in
